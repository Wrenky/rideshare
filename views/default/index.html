About Santa Cruz Rideshare:

Santa Cruz Ridesharing was developed for one thing- To connect riders to drivers! We see a problem with how people move around, each person in their own car wasting gas and potential money. SC Rideshare solves this- We allow drivers to connect to people who need rides! Drivers can offer up spaces to potential passengers, charging a price to tag along. This improves the enviroment by reducing the amount of cars on the road, while getting people where they need to go! Its a win-win situation for everybody involved.
We encorage all locals, students and professors to join together to share a resource- Cars!
